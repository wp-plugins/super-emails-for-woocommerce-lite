Promote your WooCommerce products on order confirmation emails your customers receive and improve your sells without effort.

== Description ==
With Super Emails for WooCommerce you can display your products on transactional emails sent to your customers.
Transactional emails are very important because there are the truly only ones entirely read by customers. And studies show +100% conversion rate on them.
As soon as you activate the extension, it will automatically select relative products to those added in your customers' cart and display them on the confirmation emails.

WooCommerce 2.2 minimum required

You can find more information on https://www.woosuperemails.com and get the full version which allows you to precisely select the products you want to promote
